Pizza

Description

Follow this easy and tasty pizza recipe for those short on time.

Ingredients

  • 1 ball pizza dough
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 1 package pepperoni
  • sliced mushrooms
  • parmesan cheese to taste
  • optional: green peppers, onions, olives

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees.
  2. Spread dough in grease pizza pan.
  3. Add sauce to top.
  4. Add a layer of pepperoni on top of the sauce.
  5. Add cheeses, mushrooms and optional ingredients on top.
  6. Bake in oven 14 minutes.
