Spaghetti

Description

Follow this easy and tasty spaghetti recipe for those short on time.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 1 package spaghetti noodles
  • sliced mushrooms
  • parmesan cheese to taste

Steps

  1. Brown ground beef on stove over high heat. Drain grease.
  2. Add marinara sauce and mushrooms to beef.
  3. Bring a large pot of water to boil.
  4. Boil spaghetti noodles until tender.
  5. Drain noodles and serve with sauce over top.
