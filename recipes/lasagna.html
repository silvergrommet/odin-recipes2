Lasagna

Description

Follow this easy and tasty lasagna recipe for those short on time.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 jar spaghetti sauce
  • 2 cups mozarella cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 2 teaspoons italian seasoning
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Brown ground beef in skillet. Drain grease.
  3. Stir in sauce.
  4. Combine with remaining ingredients in baking dish.
  5. Bake in oven 45 minutes.
